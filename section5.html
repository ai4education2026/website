Practical Implementation and Reflection for Teachers

  • While AI is useful for planning, material creation, text adaptation, and inspiration, it requires a critical and professional approach.
  • Pre-Task Reflection: Before using AI, identify why you are using it, the potential benefits, the risks, and what elements still require your own professional judgement.

    • Teacher Checklist:

    • Is this tool approved by my school?
    • Am I sharing any personal student data?
    • Can I verify the information?
    • Is the output fair, accurate, and age-appropriate?
    • Can I explain this use openly to students, colleagues, and guardians?
